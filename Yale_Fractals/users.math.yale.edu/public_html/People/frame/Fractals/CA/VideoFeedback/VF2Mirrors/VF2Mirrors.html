Results with Two Mirrors

Place one mirror below the monitor, the other to one side of the monitor. Aim the camera to the point at which the two mirrors touch the monitor.
Parameters include the angle and zoom of the camera, and the angles of the mirrors.
Here are several examples, all stable images. Click each picture to enlarge.
Here is a suggestion of the relation between videofeedback with mirrors and IFS. It appears that four transformations are needed:
*   Scale
*   Scale and reflect across the y-axis
*   Scale and reflect across the x-axis
*   Scale and rotate 180°
This works for some images; others require application of forbidden combinations.
