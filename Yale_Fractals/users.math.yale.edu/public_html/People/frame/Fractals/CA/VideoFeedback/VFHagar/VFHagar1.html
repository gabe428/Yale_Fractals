Results with one Mirror

David Hagar placed a mirror perpendicular to the monitor, and inclined at a shallow angle from vertical.
With this simple arrangement, he is able to generate videofeedback fractals. Here are some examples. Click each picture for a larger version.
For more examples, see his site http:// www.geocities.com/CapeCanaveral/Campus/8446/vid_fractals/index.html.
Recognizing similarities between these pictures and binary fractal trees motivated our IFS approach to simluating stable videofeedback patterns.
