1/f Noise

In terms of power spectra, 1/f noise is intermediate between white noise and Brownian noise.
Here are a sample of 1/f noise, its power spectrum, and plots of power and amplitude against frequency.
Many natural processes exhibit power spectra with 1/f characteristics. Among the examples are
*   voltage fluctuations in almost all electronic components,
*   time fluctuations in everything from hour glasses to atomic clocks,
*   traffic flow on interstates,
*   the annual flooding levels of the Nile,
*   voltages across the membranes of your nerves,
*   and even information patterns stored in DNA.
