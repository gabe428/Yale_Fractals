IFS Lab Procedure

Approach 2

* Bring up the Fractal Image Sheet on your computer.
* Place the mouse arrow over the fractal image whose IFS rules you wish to find, click the mouse button until the image is selected, and copy the image.
* Now open your graphics program and paste the image. This is the target image.
* Paste another copy and use the graphics functions - scale, rotate, reflect - to cover one of the pieces of the target. You must be careful about the placement of the pieces under rotation and reflection.
* Continue until all the pieces of the fractal image are covered.
