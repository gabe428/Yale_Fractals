The second level consists of 5 scaled copies of the first stage.
The bases of four of these copies reconstitute the base of the first stage.
The remaining copy contributes a new base, a square of area = 1/4.
Although part of this base overlaps with the top of one of the cubes of another copy, in the limiting shape that overlap reduces to a point.
So in the limiting shape, this base is indeed part of the area of the shape.
Combining levels 1 and 2 we see A2 = 1 + 1/4 = 5/4.
Whatever it is, the area of the limiting shape is at least 5/4.
