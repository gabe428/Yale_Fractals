The second level of the construction consists of 9 cubes, each of side length 1/2.
Each cube has 6 faces, each of area 1/4.
This shape consists of three scaled copies of the first level, each with 4 interior faces, hence a total of 3*4 interior faces, which do not contribute to the surface area.
In addition, these scaled copies join at 4 interior faces, which do not contribute to the surface area.
So the total surface area is
A2 = 3*(3*6*(1/4) - 4*(1/4)) - 4*(1/4)
