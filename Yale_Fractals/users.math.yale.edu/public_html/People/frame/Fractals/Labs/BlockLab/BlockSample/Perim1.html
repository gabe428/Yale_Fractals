The first level consists of 3 cubes, each of side length 1.
Each cube has 12 edges, each of length 1.
The three green edges do not contribute to the perimeter. They are edges of two cubes, so together account for 6 of the 3*12 edges not contributing to the perimeter.
The three blue edges do not contribute to the perimeter. They are edges of two cubes, so together account for 6 of the 3*12 edges not contributing to the perimeter.
The red edge does contribute to the perimeter. It is an edge of three cubes, so accounts for 2 of the 3*12 edges not contributing to the perimeter.
So the total perimeter is
P1 = 3*12*1 - 6*1 - 6*1 - 2*1 = 22
