Block Fractals

Exercises - Five Block Configuration Interpretation

We have seen the limiting shape has dimension Log(5)/Log(2) = 2.32193.
Because the dimension lies between 2 and 3, we expect
    the 1- and 2-dimensional measures are infinite and
    the 3-dimensional measure is zero.
Indeed, our computations agree with this.
The perimeter is infinite.
The area is infinite.
The volume is zero.
