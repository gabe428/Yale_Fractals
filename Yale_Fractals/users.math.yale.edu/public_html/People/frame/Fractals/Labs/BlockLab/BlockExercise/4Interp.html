Block Fractals

Exercises - Four Block Configuration Interpretation

We have seen the limiting shape has dimension Log(4)/Log(2) = 2..
Because the dimension lies between 1 and 3, we expect
    the 1-dimensional measure is infinite and
    the 3-dimensional measure is zero.
Indeed, our computations agree with this.
The perimeter is infinite.
The volume is zero.
Being a 2-dimensional measure, the area is more delicate to determine. Our calculations show the area is bounded above by 4.
