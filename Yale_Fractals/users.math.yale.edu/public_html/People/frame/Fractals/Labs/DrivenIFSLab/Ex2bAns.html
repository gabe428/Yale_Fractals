The data points alternate between bins 1 and 4, so the driven IFS points converge to points with address (14)infinity and (41)infinity.
Solving the equations T1T4(x1, y1) = (x1, y1) and T4T1(x2, y2) = (x2, y2) gives (x1, y1) = (1/3, 1/3) and (x2, y2) = (2/3, 2/3).
