Fat Cantor Set

This is a variation on the Cantor Middle Thirds Set, altering the lengths of the middle segments removed.

    The first middle cut is 1/4 the length of the long side of the paper.
    The second middle cuts are 1/4 the length of the first cut, hence 1/16 the length of the paper.
    The third middle cuts are 1/4 the length of the second cuts, hence 1/64 the length of the paper.
    In princple, continue forever. In practice, three is likely the limit of physical cuts.

This is a model of the fat Cantor set.
