Julia Sets and the Mandelbrot Set

Sample 3

The left window shows a magnification of a midget Mandelbrot set. The white diamond indicates we have selected a point in the cardioid of this midget Mandelbrot set.
In the right window, shift-click or right click near x = 0, y = 0.
We obtain a 6-cycle.
Note this midget Mandelbrot set is on the antenna above a 5-cycle disc, yet its cycle is not a multiple of 5.
