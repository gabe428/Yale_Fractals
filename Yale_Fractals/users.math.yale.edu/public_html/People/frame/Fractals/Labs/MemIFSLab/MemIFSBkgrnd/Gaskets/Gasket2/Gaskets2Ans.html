Four Gaskets

Here is the result of allowing 1 → 1 while forbidding 1 →2, 1 → 3, and 1 → 4.
Do you see why the sequence of gaskets runs off to the lower left corner?
The red gasket in square 1 comes from the allowed transitions 2 → 1, 3 → 1, and 4 → 1.
The blue gasket is T1(red gasket).
The green gasket is T1(blue gasket), and so also is T1(T1(red gasket)).
And so on.
