Fractal Wallpaper

Procedure

In creating this display we use one inch square pictures and secure them to a blank wall about ten feet long and at least six feet high.
Begin with 1,084 one inch square pictures.
Start at the left end of the wall and attach a single picture a little more than three feet off the floor.
Select a fractal pattern, for instance, one of the four examples in the Background section.
    First stage
The next five stages use the 2 by 2 squares as building blocks.
To complete the display, a total of 363 more 2 by 2 squares are needed.
