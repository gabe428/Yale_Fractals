Fractal Wallpaper

Procedure

Now combine the remaining 120 copies of the 4 by 4 squares three at a time following the fractal pattern, taping them on their backs to makle 40 copies of an 8 by 8 square, filling in the empty quadrants with blank paper.
Attach one of these 8 by 8 squares to the right of the last stage. This is stage three.
