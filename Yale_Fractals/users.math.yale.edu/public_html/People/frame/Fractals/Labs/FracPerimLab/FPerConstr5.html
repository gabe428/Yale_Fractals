Construction

The third stage is similar to the second stage.
Extend CE and HG.
Mark M on the extended HG so that GM = GE.
Draw a perpendicular at M until it intersects the extendended CE.
Call the point of intersection N.
With G as center, draw the circle through N.
Starting with N as center, mark arcs on the circle using radius GN.
