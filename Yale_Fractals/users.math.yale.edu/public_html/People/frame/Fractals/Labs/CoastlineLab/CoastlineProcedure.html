Dimension by Box-Counting

Procedure

To gain some familiarity with the mechanics of the box-counting process, we begin with a manual box-counting experiment. This is followed by an experiment using our box-counting software. We end with an exploration of the effect of different grid placement on estimating dimension.

Manual Box-Counting. Select a picture from those provided. Follow the steps on the direction sheet for manual box-counting.
Computer Box-Counting. Select a picture from the menu in the box-counting dimension program. Follow the steps on the direction sheet for computer box-counting.
Grid Placement. Select a picture from the menu in the box-counting dimension program. Follow the steps on the direction sheet for the grid placement problem.
