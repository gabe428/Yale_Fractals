Driven IFS and Financial Cartoons

Purpose To use driven IFS to detect correlations in financial data and self-affine cartoons of that data.

Materials Data sets {x1, x2, ... , xn} in sequential order (that is, the order of the entries in the list is the same as the order in which they occur) of some financial data and of some cartoon data, and some version of driven IFS software.
