L-System Geometry

For example, consider the L-system with alphabet A = {F, f}, the production rules

F -> FfF
f -> fff

and the axiom S = F. One step produces the string

FfF,

two steps produce

FfFfffFfF,

and so on. This L-system generates the Cantor middle thirds set.
