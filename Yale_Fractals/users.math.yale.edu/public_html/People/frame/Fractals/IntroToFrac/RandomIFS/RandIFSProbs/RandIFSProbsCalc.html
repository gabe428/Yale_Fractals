Probability and the Random IFS Algorithm

Very roughly speaking, the probability pi of applying Ti is the fraction of the whole attractor A occupied by Ti(A).
The area contraction factor of Ti is
Ai = risi(cos(θi)cos(φi) + sin(θi)sin(φi))
(This is just the determinant of the matrix formulation of Ti.)
Note that if θi = φi, the area contraction factor simplifies to
Ai = risi
Then the probability is given by
pi = Ai/(A1 + ... + AN)
