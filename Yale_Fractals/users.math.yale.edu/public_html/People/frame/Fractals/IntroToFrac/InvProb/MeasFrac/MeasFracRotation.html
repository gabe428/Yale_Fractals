Measuring IFS Pieces

Rotations

The green piece has been rotated.
To determine the angle of rotation, measure the angle between the green and blue rulers.
Both the left and right pictures above give 10°, so θ = 10 (left picture) and φ = 10 (right picture).
If the rotated pieces are are undistorted copies of the whole, then θ = φ, so measuring one angle suffices.
The rotations are counterclockwise, so the angles are positive.
