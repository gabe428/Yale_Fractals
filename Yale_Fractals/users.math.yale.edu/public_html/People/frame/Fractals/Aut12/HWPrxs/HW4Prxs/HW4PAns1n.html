Fourth Homework Set Answers

1. (n) This fractal consists of N = 4 pieces, with r1 = r2 = 1/2 and r3 = r4 = 1/4. The dimension, d, is the solution of the Moran equation
2(1/2)d + 2(1/4)d = 1
Taking x = (1/2)d, the Moran equation becomes the quadratic equation
2x2 + 2x - 1 = 0
The positive solution is x = (-1 + √3)/2.
Solving x = (1/2)d for d gives
d = Log((-1 + √3)/2)/Log(1/2), approximately 1.450.
