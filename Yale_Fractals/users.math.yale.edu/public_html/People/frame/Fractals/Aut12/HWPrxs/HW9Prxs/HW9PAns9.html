Ninth Homework Set Answers

9. The rule says this:
  regardless of its neighbors, every dead cell becomes alive, and
  regardless of its neighbors, every live cell dies.
Generation 1 has 24 live cells.
Generation 2 has 100 - 24 = 76 live cells.
Generation 3 has 100 - 76 = 24 live cells.
Generation 4 has 100 - 24 = 76 live cells.
Generation 5 has 100 - 76 = 24 live cells.
We see every even-numbered generation has 76 live cells and every odd-numbered generation has 24 live cells.
Consequently, generation 50 has 76 live cells.
