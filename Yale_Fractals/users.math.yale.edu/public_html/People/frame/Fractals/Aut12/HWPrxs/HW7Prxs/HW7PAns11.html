11. That the first generator is unifractal means
Log(dY1)/Log(dt1) = Log(dY2)/Log(dt2) = Log(dY3)/Log(dt3)
For the second generator, the numerators become Log(dYi2) = 2Log(dYi). Because all Log(dYi)/Log(dti) are equal, so are all 2Log(dYi)/Log(dti).
That is, the second cartoon necessarily is unifractal.
