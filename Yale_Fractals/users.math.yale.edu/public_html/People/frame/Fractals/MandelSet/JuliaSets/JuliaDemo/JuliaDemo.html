Two Julia sets

 
These are two Julia sets, fractals related to the familiar Mandelbrot set. Click each image for a sequence of magnifications.
We'll get to how these images are generated and what they mean later in the semester. For now, the issue to remember is that both images are generated from the same formula, one line long. And all the differences between the left and right images are the result of changing a single number in that formula.
