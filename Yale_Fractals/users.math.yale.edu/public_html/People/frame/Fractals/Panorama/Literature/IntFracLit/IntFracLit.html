Intentional Fractals in Literature

Fractals have appeared as explicit subjects in literature and in popular culture. In part this was to be expected, because fractals are a timely topic and some science fiction (at least) picks up current science. But fractals have had a much wider influence, which we attribute to the naturalness of the concept of scaling.
