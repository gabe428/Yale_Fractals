Graphical Iteration 1

To do this, we need to include the graph of xn+1 = xn and of xn+1 = f(xn).
To represent the dynamics of this process, we label the x-axis xn and the y-axis xn+1.
That is, if the x-coordinate of a point is one of the xn, then the y-coordinate is xn+1, the next value of the sequence.
We will represent the sequence of values x0, x1, x2, x3, ... by the sequence of points
(x0, x0)
(x1, x1)
(x2, x2)
...
