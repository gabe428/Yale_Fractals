Now S1 is orthogonal to C1, C2, C3, and C4.

Because these circles are tangent in pairs at their intersections with S1, as we have seen, the limit set L(C1, C2, C3, C4) = S1.

Similarly, L(C1, C2, C5) = S2, L(C2, C3, C5) = S3, L(C3, C4, C5) = S4, and L(C4, C1, C5) = S5.

Consequently, L(C1, C2, C3, C4, C5) contains S1, S2, S3, S4, and S5.
