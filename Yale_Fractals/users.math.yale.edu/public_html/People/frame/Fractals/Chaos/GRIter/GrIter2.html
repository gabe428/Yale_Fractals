Graphical Iteration 2

From the point x0 on the x-axis, we draw a vertical line until we intersect the graph of xn+1 = f(xn).
Points on a vertical line have the same x-coordinates, so the x-coordinate of the point of intersection is x0.
Consequently, the y-coordinate is x1 = f(x0).
That is, the vertical line intersects the graph of xn+1 = f(xn) at the point (x0, x1).
