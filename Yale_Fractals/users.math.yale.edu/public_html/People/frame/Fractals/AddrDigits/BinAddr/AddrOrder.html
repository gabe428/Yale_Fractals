Order of Addresses

Postal addresses generally go from the particular to the general:
    212 Grant Av.
    St. Albans
    WV
    USA
This is a silly order. Reading an initial part of the address tells us little: 212 Grant Av. in what city, in what state, in what counrty?
A more sensible addressing scheme is
    USA
    WV
    St. Albans
    212 Grant Av.
because then initial portions of the address do give useful information.
