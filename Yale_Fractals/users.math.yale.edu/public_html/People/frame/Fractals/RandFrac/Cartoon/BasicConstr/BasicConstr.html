Random Fractals and the Stock Market

Random Fractal Cartoons - Basic Construction

The basic construction is an instance of initiators and generators.
We take
  the initiator to be the line segment from (0,0) to (1,1), and
  the generator to be a broken line from (0,0) to (1,1) and with turning points (a,b) and (c,d).
The space and time increments are
dY1 = b - 0 = b dY2 = d - b dY3 = 1 - d
dt1 = a - 0 = a dt2 = c - a dt3 = 1 - c
The only variation on the standard initiator-generator construction is the introduction of an element of randomness, as we shall see.
