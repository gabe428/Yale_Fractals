Random Fractals and the Stock Market

Symmetric Cartoons - Why a > c is Forbidden

If a > c, then the generator no longer produces the graph of a function: some vertical lines intersect the generator at more than one point.
Recall the horizontal axis represents time and the vertical axis represents price.
Consequently, if a vertical line intersects the generator at more than one point, at the time represented by the vertical line, several prices would occur simultaneously.
