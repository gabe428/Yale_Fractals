Here is a graph of fBm with H < 1/2.
From this H, the expected value of the product of the increments is negative, so Y(t) - Y(0) and Y(t + h) - Y(t) tend to have opposite signs.
This is anti-persistent fBm.
Here H = 0.25, b = 1.2, and 50 terms were plotted.
